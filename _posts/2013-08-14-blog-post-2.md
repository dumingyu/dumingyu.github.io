I was a host for the welcome orientation for new employees.

Planning and Resource Management
======
Successfully managed event logistics under limited resources. Oversaw participant and audience management, scheduled performances, and optimizsed resource allocation to ensure smooth transitions between events.

Conflict Resolution and Team Collaboration
======
Coordinated among different performance groups to resolve scheduling and material-sharing conflicts. Negotiated last-minute replacements for participants unable to attend, ensuring the event proceeded without disruption.

Adaptability and Public Speaking
======
Demonstrated on-the-spot problem-solving skills by addressing unexpected issues such as technical difficulties or performance mishaps. Engaged the audience to maintain enthusiasm and seamlessly transitioned to subsequent segments.
